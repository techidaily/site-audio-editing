**Step 1**: **Launch to** **Add Media Files**

You can also execute “**File > Import Media > Import media files**” to bring the files into the timeline.

**Step 2**: **Edit Audio**

**Step 3**: **Adjust Volume and Add Keyframe**

**Step 3**: **Remove Background Noise**
